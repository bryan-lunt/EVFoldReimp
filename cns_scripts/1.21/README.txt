The scripts in this directory are copied from the EVFold website, modifications are made only to the names of files and to the names of input files.

Functionally, these are identical to the ones provided by the EVFold group.

You can download them anew from here:
http://evfold.org/evfold/download/StructureFromSequences_Appendix_A2.tar.gz

generate_extended.inp and generate_seq.inp are from the CNS 1.21 distribution.
